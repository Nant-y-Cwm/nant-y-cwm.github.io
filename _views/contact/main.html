Find Us

Nant-y-Cwm Steiner School is situated in a wooded valley, beside the Eastern Cleddau river. The river, which can be crossed with a single stride at it's source a few miles West, flows into the busy Milford Haven waterway and the Atlantic ocean. Nant-y-Cwm is on a minor road east of the B4313, between Llanycefn and Rhydwilym (o/s OL35 North Pembs Grid Ref. 109247).

From Carmarthen (M4)

  1. Take the A40 from Carmarthen signposted to St Clears.
  2. At Penblewin roundabout, turn right towards Cardigan on the A478
  3. Pass through Clunderwen and Llandissilio
  4. Take the left turn, signposted Llanycefn
  5. Follow road along, down and then up the hill
  6. At the 5-way Junction, take the first right, signposted Llangolman
  7. Nant-y-Cwm is approx 1 mile along this road on the left.

From Cardigan

  1. Take the A478 signposted to Tenby.
  2. Continue for approx 15 miles, passing through Crymych, Glandy Cross and Efailwen.
  3. Take the second right turn after Efailwen, signposted for Llanycefn
  4. Follow road along, down and then up the hill
  5. At the 5-way Junction, take the first right, signposted Llangolman
  6. Nant-y-Cwm is approx 1 mile along this road on the left.

From Maenclochog

  1. Take the B4313 signposted to Narberth.
  2. After approx 2 ½ miles, turn left at the crossrads, signposted Llanycefn.
  3. Follow this road down and up a steep hill.
  4. At the 5-ways crossroad turn 2nd left, signposted Llangolman
  5. Nant-y-Cwm is approx 1 mile along this road on the left.
