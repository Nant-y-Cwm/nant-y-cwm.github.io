Payment Terms

Refunds

Fees will not be refunded in the event of absence through illness, holidays, temporary absence from school, class trips, or through closure of the school due to unforeseen circumstances.

Standing order

Our preferred payment method is ten/twelve equal standing order payments per year, September to June /September to August. Fees can also be paid annually in advance no later than the first day of term, or in three termly installments no later than the first day of each term by cheque or preferably bank transfer.

Notice period

One full terms notice in writing is required if you wish to leave.

If you need any clarification on any of the above information, please contact the office.
