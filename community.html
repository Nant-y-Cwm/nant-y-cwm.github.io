Easyfundraising

We have registered with easyfundraising. It is a website from which you click through to the on-line retailer. The retailer then donates a percentage cost of the order (at no extra cost to the shopper.) Donations range from 0.5% to 20% of the purchase price or some categories give specific cash values.

It is free to use

It's a really easy way to raise funds for the school. Money raised is paid quarterly. Friends, neighbours and family might want to register too- spread the word! There are over 2000 retailers - see if the one you want is there, click through and raise some money for the school!
